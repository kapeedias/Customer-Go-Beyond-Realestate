REMOVING POTENTIAL DRAWBACKS FOR BUYERS

Beyond helps you to prevent buyers from docking dollars off their initial offer, or after their home inspection. To help with this, she hires her own professional home inspector in Chilliwack, to inspect your house before listing.

After the report is complete, Beyond provides a handyman to take care of all the minor repairs in your home, such as loose faucets or doors that no longer close properly. Afterwards, your home will be re-inspected, and all finished repairs will be removed from the report.

Beyond will let potential buyers know that they can view the report and chat with her home inspector. Oftentimes this saves buyers the extra cost of their own inspection, making putting an offer on your home even more desirable.

Beyond may suggest small cosmetic improvements or additions like planters to the front of your home, which can also help improve curb appeal and warm up viewers. When she sees fit, she will even bring in landscapers and pressure washers to freshen up the homes.
